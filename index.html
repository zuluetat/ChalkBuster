How to Use ChalkBuster

1

Pick Winners

Click a team name in any matchup to pick them as the winner. Your pick advances to the next round automatically.

2

Read the Analysis

Tap the i button on any matchup to see side-by-side stats, the Trapezoid of Excellence, pros/cons, and a confidence rating. You can also pick from inside the analysis card.

3

Navigate Matchups

Use the ← Prev / Next → buttons (or arrow keys) to flip through all matchups in a round without closing the card.

4

Understand the Stats

Tap the ? next to any metric for a plain-English definition and why it matters for your bracket. All stats are real Sports Reference data.

5

Trapezoid of Excellence

The green zone on the chart shows where championship contenders live — teams with elite efficiency at a versatile pace. If both teams are inside it, expect a dogfight.

6

Share Your Bracket

Click Share to copy a link. Anyone who opens it sees your picks in read-only mode. Your picks auto-save — just come back anytime.

What the Flags Mean

UPSET ALERT The lower seed has a real shot (30%+ win probability). Don't auto-chalk this one.

CONTRARIAN The data pick differs from public consensus. Going with the data here could separate your bracket from the pack.
